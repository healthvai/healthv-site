Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

HealthV LLC ("HealthV," "we," "us," or "our") operates the HealthV.ai platform, including the website at healthv.ai and the application at app.healthv.ai. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo via Google Sign-In.
  • Health Data: Lab results, medical documents, and health records you upload to the platform.
  • Wearable Data: Activity, heart rate, sleep, and other health metrics from Google Health Connect, with your explicit permission.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the platform, including AI chat conversations about your health data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide AI-powered analysis of your health data.
  • Enable conversations with your AI health advisor using your personal health context.
  • Display trends and insights from your lab results and wearable data.
  • Facilitate care team coordination when you invite providers.
  • Improve and maintain our services.

4. Google User Data

HealthV accesses Google user data (profile information and Google Health Connect data) solely to provide the health analysis services described in this policy. We do not use Google user data for advertising purposes. Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firebase infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Firebase Authentication for secure access control.
  • Firestore security rules that restrict data access to authenticated data owners.
  • Server-side AI processing — API keys are never exposed to the client.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your data only:

  • With care team members you explicitly invite and authorize.
  • With AI services (Google Gemini) for health analysis — your data is processed per Google's data processing terms and is not used to train AI models.
  • As required by law or to protect rights and safety.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in your account.
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Revoke access to Google Health Connect data through your Google account settings.
  • Control which care team members can view your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete all associated data within 30 days. Backups may persist for up to 90 days before automatic deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

HealthV is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
